Her Eyes!

A beautiful sculpture in front of my eyes,  
It seemed to me - a river milk white,  
With thousands of suns sparkling bright,  
The sight could banish fright.  

The light can make you sprite,  
The light can take you to the depths of ocean,  
Clear, blue and pure in it's core.  
Prejudice can never adore.  

The clarity can take you to the stars,  
The cool care of concern and smile,  
The pearl shines if your heart -  
thanks eternity for this gift.  

I always wish to see it for ever,  
For my life is dark without the light,  
Thing of beauty is a joy for ever -  
The scenario of life is beautiful and nice -  
Extant, exquisite - it was her eyes.  

25 NOV 1999

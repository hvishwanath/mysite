For Me!

The world has something for you -  
Something that is beautiful and good.  
A handful of comrades to accompany,  
A group of cynics to criticize your blood.  

A cluster of skills and some caring skulls,  
The palindrome of life has the same to say,  
Some joys and some sorrows - natural,  
Qualities like honesty, humbleness - whatever they call!  

The rainbow in every drop of water of a fountain,  
That stimulates a poetic heart - to pen!  
The world has something for our life - For me,  
A poetic heart and courage to strife.  

01 MAR 2000

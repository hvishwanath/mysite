3 Little words and tons of weight it carries...

3 Little words and it conveys meanings deeper than an ocean...

3 Little words and it has the power, the only power that can rule fear, though people think fear is the only power.

3 Little words from the person whom you are in love with and you feel you are on top of the world, your eyes are welled up with tears, ur eyes will smile rather than your lips.

3 Little words that build such strong relationships that we are ready to do anything for it.

3 Little words and its enough to tell the other person that they mean a lot to us in our lives, more than anything else, more than ourselves.


3 Little words that are responsible for the blood in millions of hearts,responsible for binding them, raising them, nourishing them, standing by them.

3 Little words that are responsible for everything in this world, including ourselves.

3 Little words that might not show you a flowery path, but will give you the strength to walk the thorns, the courage to endure and the power to reach...


3 Little words I share, I believe, I propogate and I respect.

3 Little words and so much left to unravel, to explore, to understand....

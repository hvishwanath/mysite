An ant's message...

A never ending wide surface it is,  
With lot of strange objects around me,  
Some shine bright, some cause fright,  
Some sweep me away, some bang my head.  

I get wild sometimes -  
A chaotic world around me.  
Huge shadows, high objects,  
Which seem to fall - makes me run.  

I get perplexed, I move fast,  
Some smash me away,  
Some take me under their sway.  
I persist if my luck is gay.  

I dont know what happens in the next second,  
Life is full of uncertainities - atleast make this certain,  
That you achieve in the second you spend,  
Be brave, and take care.  

27 JAN 1999

Most of us usually forget the importance of speech and the usage of right words at right time. Came accross this stuff while browsing some book.

Two smokers entered the church and started praying.

In the Middle, one of them asked, “Father, Can I Smoke when I pray”?

The Father said, “No Son. It is not allowed”

They leave the church after their prayer.

The next day they went to the church again.

This time the other friend asks the father, “Father, Can I pray when I smoke? “

The Father replied cheerfully, “Yes Son, You can”.

Know how to ask!!!

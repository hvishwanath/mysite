Barking Dogs!

Those are barking dogs - never worry.  
Who bark at your back and blink when you twink.  
Those schoffs of their words and wacks of their blunder  
will all surrender, if you dare!  

Shadows can only follow you - so are these dogs,  
If you are clear enough, you see your reflection.  
Lost the lethargic legacy lingers in the lineage,  
And the hostile hospitality locks you in a cage!  

Those valiant souls have overcome the trouble,  
The wisps of beautiy is like a glance in the bubble,  
Grab those moments bit if you lose heart,  
Those dogs can double your trouble.  

As you think so is your future,  
The gods will surrender with hearts slender,  
Those barking dogs are afraid of heights - Rise and Rise!  
Because barking dogs seldom bite!  

24 DEC 1999

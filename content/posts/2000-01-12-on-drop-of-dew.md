On a drop of dew!

There I was on a drop of dew,  
Luck permits only a few.  
Through it I could see the green hue,  
Floating on it under the sky blue.

I heard the leaf shivering with cold,  
I saw a bright aurora shimmering bold,  
I hugged the dew glittering in a ray,  
I felt the heaven in my arm's sway!

But suddenly the gusts of wind shook my head,  
The next moment I was floating...  
The morning was on with a wide gape;  
I scud on a bud of cupid's shower.

The firing rays of sun pierced my caste,  
My curio banished the scenario of pleasure;  
Good bye, I fad - fade with a sighing shoon,  
Hoping for the cool caring moon!

12 JAN 2000

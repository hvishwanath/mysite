Hiding Pain!

Why do you complain that  
Your job is difficult;  
Here's my challenge,  
Come on, hide your pain!  

Your face should be as pleasant  
as the springs of the year;  
Without a mark of pain on it  
Like a steady rock you should sit!  

It is so difficult to control your anger,  
When each of your nerve is shedding tear  
The cry that screams out of your dumb flesh;  
Can tear the superficiality of your face's mesh.  

Each second sending currents in your body,  
The volcano of pain takes it's birth,  
Not a single caring soul you can find  
On this earth which seem to be blind;  

You can never do it - I understand,  
But challenging reality - I stand!  

14 MAR 2000
